How to Use Each Feature

Welcome to the guide! Below you'll find detailed explanations and instructions on how to make the most of each feature in LeadsIQ. Let’s get started!

Quick view

The Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of your key metrics in real-time, helping you analyze your app's performance efficiently.

  • Real-Time Metrics: View live data about your traffic, sales, and conversions.
  • Efficiency Insights: Quickly assess which areas of your business are performing well and where improvements are needed.
  • Customizable Views: Tailor the dashboard to prioritize the most important metrics for your business.

Lead Wizard

Use AI-powered tools to generate high-quality leads that match your target customer profile.

  • AI-Driven Insights: Leverage machine learning to identify customers most likely to convert.
  • Lead Generation: Automatically generate new leads and add them to your pipeline.
  • Advanced Filtering: Use customizable filters to find buyers based on specific criteria, such as location, behavior, and interests.

Lead Genius

Qualify your leads more effectively using AI-powered criteria that help you identify the best prospects for your business.

  • Targeting Criteria: AI analyzes key factors such as industry, location, and behavior to qualify leads.
  • Lead Fit: Leads are matched with your ideal customer profile to ensure they are a good fit for your business.
  • Conversion Likelihood: AI ranks leads based on their likelihood of converting, helping you prioritize your outreach.

Lead Checker

Ensure the quality of your leads with AI-powered validation tools. This helps you identify which leads are most likely to convert.

  • Lead Scoring: Each lead is scored based on their potential to convert, helping you focus on high-quality prospects.
  • Real-Time Data: Validation is performed in real-time, ensuring the freshest and most accurate insights.
  • AI Recommendations: Receive personalized suggestions for improving lead quality based on AI analysis.

Competitors

Monitor your competitors and improve your conversion by analyzing their strategies and performance.

  • Competitive Analysis: Spy on competitors' offerings and strategies to stay ahead of the curve.
  • Lead Engagement: Engage with potential buyers and improve your conversion by learning from the competition.
  • Service Comparison: Evaluate your services against those of competitors to identify opportunities for improvement.

SEO Blaster

Increase your visibility on major platforms and search engines with integrated SEO tools.

  • SEO Optimization: Use advanced tools to analyze your website’s SEO and get recommendations for improvement.
  • Traffic Apps: Leverage apps designed to boost SEO across popular platforms like Google and social media.
  • Content Insights: Analyze which content is ranking well and optimize others to drive more traffic.

Social Pulse

Find key contacts and decision-makers for your business through various channels and platforms.

  • Advanced Search: Use various criteria to locate the right people for your outreach.
  • Lead Enrichment: Enhance contact profiles with additional information.
  • Data Accuracy: Ensure up-to-date and reliable contact details.

Contact Hunter

Gain insights from social media channels to drive better engagement and target the right audience.

  • Social Listening: Monitor social channels for customer sentiment and trends.
  • Audience Insights: Identify key demographics for more effective targeting.
  • Engagement Analytics: Track social media performance and optimize your strategy.

Email Blast

Reach out to multiple buyers effectively with targeted email campaigns designed for high conversion.

  • Personalized Emails: Automatically send personalized email offers to customers based on their actions.
  • Campaign Management: Schedule and manage email campaigns to send at optimal times for maximum engagement.
  • A/B Testing: Test different email versions to find what resonates best with your audience.

24/7 Help Desk

Get expert help and access to FAQs to resolve issues and maximize the results you get from the app.

  • Expert Assistance: Reach out for personalized support from our team of experts.
  • Frequently Asked Questions: Browse our comprehensive FAQ section to find answers to common issues.
  • Guides & Tutorials: Access in-depth guides and tutorials to make the most of the app's features.
